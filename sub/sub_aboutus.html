Who we are

Samsung C&T Charging Solutions

Samsung C&T Charging Solutions aims to contribute to a sustainable future by providing innovative charging technologies and solutions. We are supplying state-of-the-art EV charging stations for both the public and private sectors. As we are prioritizing our customer’s satisfaction and
positive experience, we are also offering maintenance and project management services for our deployments in the field. For customers looking for reliable,
efficient, and convenient EV charging infrastructure; we are continuously striving to become a global leader in the EV charging industry.

What we do

Samsung C&T Charging Solutions offers a comprehensive set of solutions to help our partners expand their footprint in the EV Charging industry. By partnering with qualified manufactures and service providers, we offer EV Charging solutions to fit any business application
ranging from commercial, retail, public and fleet charging stations. We have a consultative mindset to how we approach the
market and focus on constantly seeking positive outcomes for our customer’s success through valued service and support.
